About this app

   We live in tough times. A study shows that globally, 548 million people struggle with depression or anxiety. Moreover, the current global pandemic has all of us locked up in our homes, with little to none social interaction.


The benefits of journaling cannot be underestimated. Journaling has been proven to boost your mood, Enhance the sense of well-being and boost working memory(Baikie & Wilhelm, 2005). Mind Helth offers a safe, private place to express your feelings and generally dump emotions.

What we offer


A place to vent : leave your negative thoughts, your fears, your anxieties in one place, outside of your mind.

Be creative! Writing stimulates everyday creativity.

Track your mood! Get a detailed graph of how your mood evolves over time, and an analysis of which activities made you the happiest.

Track your favorite activities! Whether they be sports, reading, social interaction, find out which activities you love the most!

Get music recommended to you based on your current mood. Feeling down? Have some slow tunes to vibe to. Feeling happy? Get jazzed up with some upbeat notes!

Rest easy. Only you have access to your notes and data. Using state-of-the art military grade encryption, we encrypt your data so only you can access it. Your thoughts are yours and only yours.
